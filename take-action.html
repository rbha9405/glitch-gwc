What can you do to help?

Once you have completed an item, click it to check it off your list!

  • Step 1: Find out if there is a course in self defense that would be willing to implement itself in your school.
  • Step 2: Organize or find a petition and sign it.
  • Step 3: Consider Donating if possible.
  • Step 4: Find the School Board reps or superintendents and their contact information.
  • Step 5: Call the board member of your local school district and read the script or ammend it.
